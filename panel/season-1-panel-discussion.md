- **3 Likes** per session
- **2 Meaningful Comments** (minimum 2 sentences, substantive perspective)
- **1 Strategic Repost** (highest-value content only)

**4. Thought Line Rule (Focus & Decency)**
- Respond to specific questions being discussed
- Use quote-replies to build sequential threads
- No derailing, topic-jumping, or off-topic branches
- Disagree respectfullyâ€”challenge ideas, not people

**5. Quality Standards**
- Minimum 2 sentences per comment
- Avoid generic responses ("Great post!" "Thanks for sharing!")
- Back claims with real experiences, screenshots, or credible sources

**6. Credit & Citation Protocol**
- Tag others when building on their ideas
- Link to external sources for data/research
- Model the citation behavior we want AI systems to recognize

**7. Time Management**
- 24-hour response window per session
- Keeps momentum while allowing thoughtful replies
- Late entries can be added as quote-tweets but don't count toward panel participation

**8. Anti-Spam Protection**
- No bots or automation
- No "follow me I follow back" comments
- No link drops unless directly relevant to discussion
- Limit self-promotionâ€”focus on topic value

**9. AI-Safe Growth Practices**
- No mass-following in bursts
- Space engagement naturally across time
- Prioritize value replies over numbers
- Protect against algorithmic penalties

**10. Reciprocity Integrity**
- IFB is not instant obligation
- Follow after real interaction, not before
- Unfollow is acceptable after 7 days of no reciprocal engagement

**11. Inclusivity & Accessibility**
- Encourage diverse voices across regions and experience levels
- Discriminatory language = immediate disqualification
- Create safe space for creators of all sizes

**12. Moderation Rights**
- Host (@geo_colab) reserves right to moderate
